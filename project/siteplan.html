Artisan Haven

Local Artisans Marketplace Site Plan

Site Name

Artisan Haven

Description: This name was chosen to evoke a welcoming space for creators and shoppers alike. "Artisan" clearly communicates our focus on skilled craftsmanship, while "Haven" suggests a safe, reliable marketplace for unique handmade goods.

Domain Availability: artisan-haven.com (available for registration)

Site Purpose

Artisan Haven serves two primary purposes:

For Artisans

  • Showcase handmade products
  • Reach new customers
  • Manage inventory and sales
  • Build professional profiles

For Shoppers

  • Discover unique local goods
  • Connect with makers
  • Support small businesses
  • Find ethically-made products

User Scenarios

Gift Shopper

"I need a special birthday present and want something unique from local makers. How can I filter products by price range and delivery options?"

New Artisan

"I'm a potter looking to sell online. What's the process for setting up my shop and what fees are involved?"

Returning Customer

"I bought a wooden bowl last month - can I find the same artisan to see their new collection?"
